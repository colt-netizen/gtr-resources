## Interactive Tools

- **[GTM ROI Calculator](https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/colt-netizen/gtr-resources/blob/main/roi-calculator.html)** - See how much you're wasting on GTM. Input your spend, get your efficiency score and potential savings.

## What These Do

✅ **GTM ROI Calculator** - Input annual revenue, marketing spend, sales spend. Get back: wasted spend %, potential savings, revenue opportunity, and diagnosis.

✅ **GTM Framework** - One-page clarity on your go-to-market. Covers: ICP, positioning, channel, acquisition, activation, pricing, metrics.

Both are fully functional web tools. No signup, no tracking, just useful.
