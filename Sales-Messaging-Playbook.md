## Part 2: The Core Positioning (One Sentence)

**How do you frame the solution?**

For [target customer]
who [problem/situation]
we provide [category/positioning]
that [key benefit they care about]
unlike [alternative]
we [key differentiator]

**Example:**
For B2B SaaS founders
who struggle to build repeatable go-to-market systems
we provide GTM consulting and strategy
that gives them predictable revenue growth
unlike generic marketing agencies
we focus on systems and repeatability over tactics

## How to Use This

1. **Fill it out as a team** (30 min)
2. **Salespeople practice it** (out loud, not written)
3. **Test it with real customers** (record yourself)
4. **Refine based on what works** (quarterly)

---

## Quick Audit: Does Your Messaging Work?

- [ ] Can you say it in 30 seconds without notes?
- [ ] Does it lead with the PROBLEM, not your solution?
- [ ] Is it different from how competitors talk?
- [ ] Does it make someone want to learn more?
- [ ] Can a junior person say it and sound believable?

If you can't check all 5, go back and edit.
