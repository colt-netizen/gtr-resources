# ICP Qualification Checklist

**Score your prospect in 5 minutes. Know if it's worth chasing.**

---

## Company Fundamentals (20 points)

- [ ] **Right industry** (matches your ICP) — 5 points
- [ ] **Right company size** (we actually close here) — 5 points
- [ ] **Right funding stage** (can afford us) — 5 points
- [ ] **Not a competitor** (we shouldn't work with them) — 5 points

**Subtotal: ___/20**

---

## Problem Fit (20 points)

- [ ] **Has the problem we solve** (GTM, sales, growth, etc.) — 5 points
- [ ] **Problem is actually causing them pain** (they mention it) — 5 points
- [ ] **They've tried to solve it before** (not a new problem) — 5 points
- [ ] **They're actively looking for help** (not "future consideration") — 5 points

**Subtotal: ___/20**

---

## Decision Making (15 points)

- [ ] **You're talking to the decision maker** (CEO, COO, CMO, etc.) — 5 points
- [ ] **They have budget authority** (not just influence) — 5 points
- [ ] **Timeline to decision is clear** (not indefinite) — 5 points

**Subtotal: ___/15**

---

## Budget (15 points)

- [ ] **Can afford our price point** ($X-$Y/month) — 5 points
- [ ] **Budget is approved or can be approved soon** (not "maybe next quarter") — 5 points
- [ ] **They've spent on similar services before** (not price-shopping) — 5 points

**Subtotal: ___/15**

---

## Opportunity & Timing (15 points)

- [ ] **Project timeline matches our delivery** (not rushed, not indefinite) — 5 points
- [ ] **They're serious** (not just "picking your brain") — 5 points
- [ ] **Win probability is real** (we've done this 10+ times before) — 5 points

**Subtotal: ___/15**

---

## SCORING

**Total: ___/80**

**Scoring Guide:**
- **70-80:** Pursue aggressively. This is a qualified lead.
- **60-69:** Good prospect. Move forward, but not top priority.
- **50-59:** Maybe. See if something changes to move them up.
- **Below 50:** Pass. Not the right fit.

---

## Questions to Ask to Score Accurately

**Fundamentals:**
- What's your company size?
- What stage are you? (Revenue? Funding?)
- What industry are you in?

**Problem Fit:**
- What's your biggest challenge right now? (GTM? Sales? Growth?)
- How long have you been struggling with this?
- Have you tried other solutions? What didn't work?

**Decision Making:**
- Who else needs to be involved in this decision?
- Do you have budget approved for this type of work?
- When would you want to start?

**Budget:**
- What's your budget range for solving this?
- Have you worked with consultants before? What did you spend?

**Opportunity:**
- What would success look like for you?
- What's your timeline?
- Are you comparing us to other options?

---

## Red Flags (Pass Immediately)

Even if score is high, pass if:
- [ ] "We just want to pick your brain" (not buying)
- [ ] "We need to talk to 5 other people" (no authority)
- [ ] "We'll revisit this next year" (not real timeline)
- [ ] "We're price shopping" (not serious)
- [ ] "We want free work to test you" (bad fit)
- [ ] "We'll let you know in 6 months" (time waster)

---

## Green Flags (Move Fast)

If they have these, prioritize:
- [ ] Decision maker is personally driving this
- [ ] They mention a specific problem (not vague)
- [ ] They say "we need help NOW"
- [ ] They ask about your approach, not just price
- [ ] They've mentioned they've worked with consultants before

---

## What to Do With Score

**70+:** Follow up TODAY. This is hot.
**60-69:** Follow up this week. Nurture in pipeline.
**50-59:** Keep in list. Reach out quarterly.
**Below 50:** Archive and move on.

---

## How to Use This

1. **Have discovery conversation**
2. **Fill this out immediately after** (while conversation is fresh)
3. **Share with team** (should everyone agree on score?)
4. **Make follow-up decision** (pursue? nurture? pass?)

---

## Pro Tip

The score is just a guide. Your gut matters too.

If something feels off, it probably is.
If something feels right, lean into it.

But use the checklist to override your bias when needed.
