## What to Do With The Data

1. **Export to CSV** (Apollo → Actions → Download)
2. **Clean data:**
   - Remove: Consultants, agencies, other service providers
   - Keep: Software, SaaS, fintech, healthcare tech, etc.
3. **Prioritize:**
   - Recently funded (funding data in Apollo)
   - Recently hired (headcount growth = pain)
   - Job title changed recently
4. **Use Copy Skill** to write personalized cold emails
5. **Track in Pipedrive:**
   - Add prospects to pipeline
   - Track opens, clicks, replies
   - Follow up sequence (3 emails max)

## Quick Win Plays

### Play 1: Vertical Specific (Higher Reply Rates)
Don't search "Series A SaaS"
Search: "Series A Fintech" OR "Series A Healthcare Tech"
Specific vertical = higher relevance = higher reply rates

### Play 2: Recent Funding Signal
Apollo shows funding date
Prioritize companies that closed funding <3 months ago
They're actively hiring, building GTM = pain is fresh

### Play 3: Recently Hired (Hiring Signal)
Companies that added 5+ people = scaling
Use Copy Skill: "I saw you're scaling team" = immediate relevance

### Play 4: Geographic Clustering
Focus on one city/region first (SF Bay, NYC, Austin)
Easier to get warm intros
Higher likelihood they know each other/mutual connections
